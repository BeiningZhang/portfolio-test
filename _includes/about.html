About

Beining Zhang is a prospective PhD student in the Agents, Interaction and Complexity (AIC) research group at the University of Southampton. She holds a BSc and MSc in Mathematics, Operational Research, Statistics, and Economics (MORSE) from the University of Warwick. Her research focuses on Artificial Intelligence (AI), specifically autonomous agents (AA) and multi-agent systems (MAS), and how they can help humans resolve major cooperation challenges.
